What is Stacked?

Are you tired of standing in front of a library shelf with no idea of what to read? Have you lost all of your tiny slips of paper with titles suggested by friends? Is your desk cluttered with book reviews or bestseller lists? Do you want to keep track of all the books your club selected for the year?

Create a Virtual Stack

Stacked allows you to create your own custom list of titles, so that you are never again without a "next good book." If you need a recommendation, search by a favorite author's name or a keyword. If you only know a title fragment, Stacked can help you find all the details.

Keep Track of Your Stack

Stacked also allows you to keep track of your list. You can add and delete titles to and from your Stack, or mark items as "read," if you'd like to keep track of your reading progess.
